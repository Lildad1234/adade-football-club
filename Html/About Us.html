Brief History About Adeda football Club

Daniel Adade established a division team with over 60 players with huge expectations of giving life to young footballers and nurturing them. Football has grown to become a business entity and the Ghanaian footballer has taken the step to venture into club administration. The deadly striker who ply's his trade currently in the Liga de Elite with Benfica de Macau is now a club owner and administrator at just age 29. Having passed through the difficult path of becoming a professional, has the aim to unearth young players and offer them a good platform to achieve their goals in football.

The Aim of Adeda Football Club

To breath life into young Ghanaian footballers with aim of travelling abroad to play professional football. And again, provide footballing environmment that can guide their growth.
